Click on a field and you'll see how many bombs surrounds it.

If you think there's a bomb somewhere, mark the field with a flag.

To win, you must mark all the bombs and uncover all the fields.

Left mouse button uncover field
Right mouse button mark field
Shift + Left mouse button uncover all fields adjacent to the one alredy uncovered
Tap the field to uncover it
Hold it to mark it
